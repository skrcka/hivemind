## Accelerators & Programs

### StartupYard Remote Lab
**Link:** https://startupyard.com/apply-to-startupyard-remote-lab/

Prague-based accelerator running since 2011. Offers a **remote 30-hour business and fundraising program** for pre-revenue and early-revenue tech startups. Focus areas include Deep-Tech and FinTech.

**What they provide:**
- Business planning and refinement
- Mentorship from a network of highly connected mentors
- Introductions to investors
- Business connections across the region

**Track record:** 113 startups accelerated, portfolio has collectively raised over €100M.

**Good fit if:** we are pre- or early-revenue and looking for structured fundraising preparation and investor introductions.
